Meet Our Founder

JERALD

Pushpam Group is a trusted provider of innovative services across catering, real estate, and facility management. We focus on quality, efficiency, and customer satisfaction in every service we offer. Our catering division delivers hygienic, tasty, and nutritious meals to factories, schools, offices, and events. We prioritize cleanliness, timely delivery, and customer health, earning trust across Tamil Nadu. In real estate, we develop functional and well-designed hostels, PGs, and commercial buildings. We ensure transparency, quality construction, and on-time completion in all our projects. Our facility management services include housekeeping, sanitation, maintenance, and staff support. We help organizations run smoothly by handling essential services professionally. Our skilled team blends experience with innovation and modern tools. We are committed to delivering the best results with care and dedication. Pushpam Group is known for reliability, integrity, and strong client relationships. We focus on sustainable practices that create long-term value. Our mission is to be a dependable partner in every client’s success.
